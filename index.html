I'm a Staff Product Manager at GoodLeap, where I lead the product strategy for a 100+ MW Virtual Power Plant (VPP) aggregating 15,000+ home batteries across the United States. My work sits at the intersection of energy markets, grid technology, and consumer software.

My career spans across NRG Energy, GM Energy/General Motors, Fluence Energy, and Entergy — from incubating new businesses to scaling distributed energy platforms to enabling and extracting recurring revenue from $10B+ in projects financed

    I have worked on VPPs since 2018, across device types from:
  • 500 kW Natural Gas Generators for commercial facilities at Entergy
  • Optimizing Smart Thermostat demand response at NRG
  • Building out and leading Electric Vehicle managed charging at GM
  • Global deployments of Utility-Scale BESS at Fluence Energy
  • Today at GoodLeap, I am the product lead for a rapidly growing virtual power plant focused on residential batteries and expanding into other distributed energy resource types.

I hold an MSc in Innovation & Entrepreneurhsip from HEC Paris, a BS in Economics from Texas A&M University, and am a proud Clean Energy Leadership Institute (CELI) Fellow.

Outside the office: Marathoner, Eagle Scout, Book Collector & Reader of 50+ books a year for the last decade, and a tinkerer across AI tools and side projects.
